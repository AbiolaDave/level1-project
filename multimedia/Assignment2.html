A Guide To FUNAAB

The Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta, located at Alabata, is a science school that offers a wide varieties of science courses which are not limited to agriculture courses alone.

Colleges in FUNAAB And Departments Under It

  • College of Enviromental Resources Management(COLERM)

    • Department of Aquaculture and fisheries Management.
    • Department of Enviromental Management and Toxicology.
    • Forestry and Wildlife Management.
    • Water Resoures Management and Agrometeorology.

  • College of Animal Science and Livestock Management(COLANIM).

    • Animal Breeding and Genetics.
    • Animal Production and Health.
    • Animal Physiology.
    • Pasture and Range Management

  • College of Physiscal Science(COLPHYS).

    • Computer Science
    • Chemistry
    • Physics
    • Statistic
    • Mathematics

  • College Of Biological Science(COLBIOS)

    • Biochemistry
    • Microbiology
    • Applied Zoology
    • Applied Botany
